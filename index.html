Career Profile

During my post-graduate studies, researching particle physics at the University of Liverpool I performed complex data analysis with one of the world’s most advanced experiments at CERN, Geneva. During this work, I developed a keen interest in data and a deep understanding of statistical and analytical techniques. I now work in developing novel Data Science (DS) products with Elastacloud, learning to leverage advanced Machine Learning (ML) techniques to enable us to utilise unstructured data sets. I have thoroughly enjoyed the creative and challenging nature of unstructured data, and have excelled with this opportunity to learn and develop my skill-set. Both my doctoral research and my time with Elastacloud have been thoroughly interesting, varied and educational experiences, that have lead me to become a highly skilled and competent Data Scientist.
